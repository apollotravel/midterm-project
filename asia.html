Hong Kong

Hong Kong, Hong Kong

Tian Tan Buddha: 112 foot bronze statue of the Buddha on Lantau Island.

Victoria Lookout: Lush lookout spot with telescopes for a beautiful view of the HK skyline.

Ngong Ping 360: Gondola lift ride providing stunning aerial views of Lantau Island.

The Peak: Visit for the picturesque view of HK at 1400 feet high.



Mumbai

Mumbai, India

Gateway of India: Arch-monument built in the twentieth century.

Jehangir Art Gallery: Serves as the hub for many Indian artists.

Bandra-Worli Sea Link: Cable bridge that links the Western Suburbs of Mumbai with South Mumbai.

Kamala Nehru Park: Luscious gardens in India covering over 16,000 square meters.









Seoul

Seoul, South Korea

Lotte World Tower: Tallest building in South Korea, measuring over 1800 feet with a 360 degree view.

Dongdaemun Design Plaza: Iconic urban development landmark characterized by its neofuturistic design.

Banpo Bridge: Overtop the Han River. The first double-deck bridge in South Korea.

Gyeongbokgung Palace: Main royal palace in northern South Korea.

Tokyo

Tokyo, Japan

Imperial Palace: Large park-like structure where the Japan's Imperial Family resides.

Meiji Jingu: Shinto shrine dedicated to the deified spirits of Emperor Meiji and his wife, Empress Shōken.

Harajuku: Renowned center for colorful street art and cosplay shops along Takeshita Street.

Tsukiji Market: Sprawling fish market with popular tuna auctions.
